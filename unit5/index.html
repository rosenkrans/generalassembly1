MARK'S SQUASH CASSEROLE

  • 6-7 yellow squash
  • 2 Cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 Stacks Ritz Crackers
  • 1 1/2 Cups milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 stick butter, melted

Cook squash until mushy and drain

In a mixing bowl, add: squash, 1 1/2 cups of shredded chese, 1 stack of Ritz cracker crumbs, milk, eggs, pepper, 2/3 stick melted butter. Mix well. Pour into a greased backing dish. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup of cheese, 1 stack of cracker crumbs, and 1/3 stick of melted butter. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes or until golden brown

BROILED TOMATOES AU GRATIN

  • 3 large tomatoes, cut into ½” slices
  • ½ lb bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • ¼ lb shredded swiss or mozzarella
  • 1 T dried basil
  • 2 T dried parsley
  • salt and freshly ground pepper

Cook bacon until slightly crisp, drain and set aside. Saute onions in that pan until soft. Mix the bacon, onion, cheese, and spices. Preheat the broiler. Arrange the tomatoes on an oiled broiler pan and sprinkle the top with salt and pepper. Top each tomato slice with some of the bacon mixture and broil the slices just until the cheese melts. Serve hot. Makes about 8 servings

(unless you love them as much as we do, then, about 6).
